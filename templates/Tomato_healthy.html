Tomato is Healthy

Tomatoes grow best in a spot with full sun and well-drained soil. They'll be more prone to disease if you plant them in heavy clay soil or shade. Also, it's helpful to plant tomatoes in a different part of the garden each year as diseases can build up in the soil.

Most tomatoes do really well in large pots filled with a potting mix designed for container-grown plants. Just be sure you have a pot that's at least 12 inches in diameter; many tomato varieties can grow 6 feet tall by the end of the season!
